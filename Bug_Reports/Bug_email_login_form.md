#### Bug ID: BR\_UI\_002 Title: Login Form Allows Invalid Emails



**Environment:** Chrome, Windows 10



**Steps to Reproduce:**

1\. Open the login page

2\. Enter an invalid email like `user@` or `test.com`

3\. Enter any password

4\. Click the "Login" button



**Actual Result:** The form accepts the invalid email and proceeds with login.



**Expected Result:** The form should show an error message and prevent login.



**Severity:** High  

**Priority:** High
